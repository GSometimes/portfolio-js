Works.

What I've made.

My primary area of interest when it comes to development and design is the frontend. When it comes to creating, I've primarily worked with React, but I'm always looking to learn and develop new skills. Here is a small gallery of projects that I've made individually or with a small team.

CinePrime

CinePrime is a MERN stack application that was designed to create a TV and movie watch list. It was a team project in which I contributed to the wire-framing and frontend design. View CinePrime.

Bath Bomb Shop

Bath Bomb Shop is a Shopify storefront template that can be used for a variety of product storefronts. Created with React, ChakraUI, and Shopify API. View Storefront.

Little Black Book

Little Black book was designed as a personal blog template that displays entries in a unique, aniamted way. Created with React, Contentful, and CSS Keyframes. View Little Black Book.

Abode by Sometimes

Abode by Sometimes is a mock Realtor Website that showcases properties for sale and for rent all over the world. Created with React, Styled Components, Framer Motion and Animate on Scroll. View Abode.

About.

Who I am.

Hi, I'm Gore. I am a Chef turned Frontend Developer who is taking my creativity from plate to page while exploring this new world of design and development. I'm a father, puppy parent, foodie, and gamer who's always looking to make new memories with family and friends.

Simple ideas, modern design, hand-crafted experiences. From plates to pages, I build a foundation utilizing simple ingredients that incorporates all the senses resulting in a hand-crafted experience, making a lasting impression.
